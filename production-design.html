College of San Mateo's Wellness Center Brochure

About the Project

Tri-fold brochure created for College of San Mateo's Wellness center. The whole ide was to showcase the mental heatlh services they offer, so students can be aware of all the help they can get for free. I used the color palette and the font recomended by the College of San Mateo's styles book. I tried to play with circles and rounded figures to make it look more friendly and fun. Artwork created using both Adobe InDesign and Illustrator.
