Communication Arts Magazine Layout

About the Project

Editorial design about English photographer Damon Baker. He is known for his black and white iconic portraits of Hollywood superstars. I decided to use black and white colors with a dash of red to give my design a powerful look. I wanted to showcase his majestic work, so I decided to have a simple layout where his portraits were the main focus.
